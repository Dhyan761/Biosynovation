about us

Delivering Inovation to Science

Biosynovation Technologies is an innovative biotechnology startup focused on creating niche products that make complex devices approachable for research purposes. The start up aims to deliver research convince by developing novel products that provides accessibility , affordability and efficiency to the user working on day to day molecular biology experiments.

Working Principle

How It Works

The device is an all-in-one product that works as an illuminator, AGE, and PAGE, which is primarily used to create a potential difference to separate biologically active compounds such as DNA, RNA, and proteins. The device is capable of providing in situ observation of AGE and PAGE with UV illumination. The tube can be replaced with a fluorescence tube to record the results of fluorescence tagging.

Advantages

Why Choose Electroluminator

  • High energy efficiency with minimal power usage.
  • Consistent and even illumination across the sample area.
  • Compact, portable, and easy to use for all researchers.
  • Cost-effective compared to traditional alternatives.
  • Durable, with long operational lifespan in lab conditions.
